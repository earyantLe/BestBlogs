## 中文

### ✨ 新功能

- OpenAPI 用户新增批量取消关注订阅源能力：通过 CLI 执行 `bestblogs intake unfollow --source-ids <id>` 即可批量移除订阅，未关注的源自动跳过，与关注操作完全对称

### 🔧 体验优化

- 播客和视频详情页内容概要移至字幕区之后展示，收听流程从「播放器 → 章节 → 字幕 → 概要 → 节目说明」更顺畅

### 🐛 修复

- 播客和视频详情页切换章节时，页面不再自动滚回顶部，章节列表在自身区域内平滑居中定位

## English

### ✨ New Features

- OpenAPI users can now batch-unfollow sources via `bestblogs intake unfollow --source-ids <id>` in the CLI; sources you haven't followed are silently skipped, completing the follow/unfollow symmetry

### 🔧 Improvements

- Podcast and video detail pages now place the content overview after the transcript, creating a more natural flow: player → chapters → transcript → overview → show notes

### 🐛 Fixes

- Switching chapters in podcast and video detail pages no longer scrolls the whole page back to the top; the chapter list now smoothly centers the active chapter within its own panel
