## 中文

### ✨ 新功能

- 世界杯签到新增「比赛日加成」：当天有比赛时签到可额外获得金球币奖励，场次越多奖励越高
- 世界杯任务刷新：浏览今日早报、查看精选周刊、添加 3 个订阅源，分别可得 500/500/1000 金球币

### 🔧 体验优化

- 比分速览与全部比赛改为按开球时间倒排，最新比赛优先看到
- 关注源推荐更贴合你在 Timeline 里实际感兴趣的体育内容
- 下注锁定后点击「继续下注」直接回到比赛列表，方便连续给多场下注
- 预测列表「查看下一个比赛日」一次加载完整一天的比赛，不再固定展开 5 场
- 「我的战绩」战绩列表新增真实比分展示，命中/未中颜色区分更清晰
- 排行榜精简展示，去掉冗余的徽标和说明文字
- onboarding 完成页新增 Apple Podcast 关注入口，公众号与官方 X 账号信息同步更新

### 🐛 修复

- 早报候选内容不再混入未订阅的私有来源，避免点开报错

## English

### ✨ New Features

- World Cup check-in now includes a match-day bonus: check in on a day with matches to earn extra GC, more matches means a bigger bonus
- Refreshed World Cup tasks: browse today's Brief, check the Weekly Digest, and follow 3 sources, worth 500/500/1000 GC

### 🔧 Improvements

- Score glance and the full match list are now sorted by kickoff time, newest first
- Followed-source recommendations now better match the sports content you actually engage with on the Timeline
- After locking in a pick, tap Continue Betting to head straight back to the match list and keep predicting
- The prediction list's Next Match Day button loads a full day of matches at once instead of a fixed batch of 5
- My Record now shows the real final score for each settled pick, with clearer win/loss coloring
- The leaderboard is more compact, with redundant badges and labels removed
- The onboarding completion screen adds an Apple Podcast entry, plus updated WeChat and official X account info

### 🐛 Fixes

- The Brief candidate pool no longer surfaces unsubscribed private sources, preventing broken links when opening the Brief
