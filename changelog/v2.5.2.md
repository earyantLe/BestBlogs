## 中文

### ✨ 新功能

- 世界杯专刊详情页区分赛前和赛后两种视图：赛前展示普神决策依据和可下注赔率，赛后展示战报回顾、普神复盘与注单对账、大众投票分布
- 每场比赛可以提前投票支持你看好的队伍，封盘前可以修改，结算后查看对账结果

### 🔧 体验优化

- 精选周刊邮件条目升级：每篇文章附带来源、日期、星级评分和推荐理由，不用打开详情页就能快速判断是否值得读
- 早报、文章详情页和内容广场首屏数据改为服务端加载，页面打开更快，内容不再先空白后跳入

### 🐛 修复

- 精选周刊详情页所有文章封面统一为 2:1 比例，竖图不再被裁成一条细长条

## English

### ✨ New Features

- World Cup Special detail pages now have two distinct views: a pre-match view with the Prophet's rationale and betting odds, and a post-match view with the match recap, Prophet's review, bet settlement, and fan vote distribution
- Vote on any match before kickoff to back the team you believe in; you can change your pick before the deadline and see how it compares after the final whistle

### 🔧 Improvements

- Curated newsletter emails now include source, date, star rating, and recommendation reason for each article, so you can decide what to read without opening the full issue
- Brief, article detail, and Explore pages now load first-screen data on the server, so content appears immediately instead of loading in after a blank state

### 🐛 Fixes

- All article cover images in newsletter detail pages are now uniformly cropped to 2:1, eliminating the awkward thin-strip look for portrait photos
