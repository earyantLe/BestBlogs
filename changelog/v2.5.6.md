## 中文

### 🔧 体验优化

- 精选周刊：周刊详情页和邮件加入主题图，重点文章以视觉精选呈现，并补充最近几期入口，阅读和回看都更直观。
- Pro 早报：非 Pro 用户访问早报时会直接看到升级提示，不再经历无效加载或异常提示。

### 🐛 修复

- X 关注源：英文用户名但主要发布中文内容的账号，现在能更准确识别为中文源，后续推荐和筛选更贴近真实内容语言。

## English

### 🔧 Improvements

- Featured Weekly: weekly pages and emails now include a theme banner, visual highlights for key articles, and quick links to recent issues for easier reading and revisiting.
- Pro Brief: non Pro users now see the upgrade prompt directly instead of going through a failed loading state or confusing error.

### 🐛 Fixes

- X Sources: accounts with English profile names but mostly Chinese original posts are now recognized more accurately as Chinese sources, improving later recommendations and filtering.
